What Household Object Are You?


Ever though about what household item you'd be? Or have you ever looked at someone and decided, "Yep -- they're an air vent"? Just me? Alright...

Anyway, this quiz is meant to show you what you might turn into if you were ever turned into an item by a not-so-friendly-wizard. There's 8 options regarding what you could be (according to this quiz):

  • Blender
  • Microwave
  • Rug
  • Lamp
  • Plant
  • Clock
  • Fan
  • Shelf

While I can't guarantee your results are accurate, since the chances of me turning you into an object is fairly slim, I can guarantee that you'll have fun while taking the quiz! Or atleast...
